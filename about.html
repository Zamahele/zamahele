About us

From rural roots to real impact: technology that empowers people and business.

Our story

Zama grew up in a rural area where access to computers and the internet was scarce. That experience created a conviction: technology is life — a powerful enabler that can open doors for families and communities. BLACK TECH EMPOWER exists to build practical software and to champion inclusion so that more black brothers and sisters can thrive in the digital economy.
